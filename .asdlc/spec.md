# Overview

An internal leave management application where WSO2 employees request time off and managers approve or decline those requests.

# Personas

- Employee — requests leave and tracks their balance
- Manager — reviews and approves leave requests from their team
- HR administrator — manages company leave policies and balances

# Features

- An employee submits a leave request by selecting dates and a reason.
- Managers see pending requests from their direct reports and can approve or reject them.
- Employees view their remaining leave balance by type (annual, sick, etc.).
- HR sets the initial leave allocation for each employee at the start of the year.
- Everyone can see a calendar view showing who's out and when.
